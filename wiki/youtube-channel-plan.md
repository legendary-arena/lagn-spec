## Summary

The YouTube channel ("Legendary Arena") is the transitional CTA the
homepage needs. It serves all five Player Needs Pyramid levels and all
three SB7 content modes (Sales, Narrative, Authority) through four
series:

| Series | Content Mode | Pyramid Levels | Cadence |
|--------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Building the Arena (dev log) | C (Authority) | L2 Trust + L3 Community | Biweekly |
| How to Play (tutorials) | A/C (Sales/Authority) | L1 Access + L5 Mastery | Front-load 5-8, then as features ship |
| Across the Table (interview + gameplay) | B (Narrative) | L3 Community + L4 Recognition | Phased: solo weeks 1-4, guest week 5+ |
| Arena Clips (Shorts) | All | All (discovery layer) | 3-5 per week |

## Core Principles

1. **One Video = One Goal** — each video declares exactly one primary
   goal (play-conversion, email-signup, or subscriber-growth)
2. **Packaging Before Production** — title, thumbnail, and topic demand
   validated before recording
3. **Retention Before Volume** — fix retention first, then scale output
4. **Shorts = Testing Engine** — Shorts test hooks and topics; top
   performers become long-form candidates
5. **Session > Single Video** — every end screen links to the next
   video in the binge path

## SB7 Framework for Video

Every video employs the seven StoryBrand elements:

- **Character + Desire:** The viewer is the hero. Single desire:
  "A card game that rewards your brain, not your wallet."
- **Villain + Three Problem Levels:** The pay-to-win model. Every video
  touches at least one level (external, internal, philosophical).
- **Guide (Empathy + Authority):** Host is Yoda, not Luke.
  Trust = Empathy + Authority.
- **The Plan:** Process plan (Build → Play → Earn) + Agreement plan
  (Fair Play Promise).
- **Call to Action:** Direct (identity CTA) + Transitional (next video)
  + Email (lead magnet). Mid-video CTA at peak value delivery.
- **Failure Stakes + Success Vision:** What happens if you don't act
  vs. what life looks like when you do.
- **Identity Transformation:** Frustrated player → Respected competitor.

## Success Criteria

The system is working when these trends improve over a rolling 30-day
window:

- CTR improving over baseline
- Avg view duration increasing
- Shorts views increasing
- Email signups increasing
- Play conversions increasing
- Production time per video decreasing
- Session watch time increasing
